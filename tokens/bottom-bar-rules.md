Bottom Bar Rules:

Purpose:
- Drive final action (conversion)

Types:
- Row → multiple stacked actions
- Column → primary + secondary actions
- Price → checkout / payment

Guidelines:
- Always show 1 clear primary CTA
- Avoid more than 2 actions
- Use sticky positioning
- Keep padding sufficient for thumb reach

Usage:
- Checkout → price + CTA
- Forms → submit action
- Booking → confirm action
- AI → next step CTA
